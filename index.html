TAROT

Tarot is built upon a blend of symbolism, numerology, Kabbalistic mysticism, and astrology. Within its 78 cards lies the reflection of the human soul’s journey — from the beginning of awareness to inner awakening and transformation. Every number, color, and symbol on the cards resonates with universal energy and cosmic rhythm. Tarot is a bridge between the science of the human psyche and the ancient wisdom of the cosmos — a tool for discovering meaning, intuition, and harmony between the mind, the soul, and the energy of the universe.

LENORMAND

The Lenormand card deck originated around 1800 in France and is named after Madame Marie Anne Adélaïde Lenormand, the legendary fortune-teller of Napoleon and Josephine. This deck consists of 36 symbolic cards, each carrying clear and inspiring messages about one’s life path, relationships, and personal choices. Unlike the Tarot, which focuses more on symbolic, inner, and spiritual aspects, Lenormand offers a direct reflection of everyday reality — cards that provide deep insight and guidance about decisions, relationships, career paths, and even the near future with remarkable clarity.

POKER

In Poker Divination, the symbols, colors, and numbers of the cards are used to receive psychological and spiritual messages. This method is based on the connection between numerology, psychology, and inner energies, and while it shares similarities with Tarot and Lenormand, it is a simpler, faster, and highly intuitive approach. With fewer cards, it can reveal deep and precise insights about one’s inner state, decisions, and spiritual path — a quality that makes it a truly unique tool for understanding energies and accessing the subconscious mind.
