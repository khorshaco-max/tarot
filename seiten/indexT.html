About Tarot

Tarot cards were invented in 15th-century Europe, particularly in Italy, as an aristocratic card game.
Until the 18th century, they had no connection to divination.

Around 1780 (about 250 years ago), a French mystic named Etteilla was the first to use them for fortune-telling.

Since then, tarot has gradually evolved from a simple game into a mystical and spiritual tool.
