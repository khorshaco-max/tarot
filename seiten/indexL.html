About Lenormand

Lenormand cards originated in early 19th-century France. They were named after the famous French fortune-teller Madame Marie Anne Adélaïde Lenormand.
first, they were created as a card game, but after her death, they gradually began to be used for divination and fortune-telling.
Today, Lenormand is one of the most popular divination systems in Europe.
