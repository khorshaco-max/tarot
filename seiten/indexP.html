About Poker

The use of regular playing cards for divination began in Europe around the 16th century.
Before Tarot and Lenormand became popular, people used ordinary cards to interpret symbols and predict the future.
During the 18th and 19th centuries, this practice—known as cartomancy—spread widely in France and England. Cartomancy is still one of the traditional methods of fortune-telling using a standard 52-card deck.
